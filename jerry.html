About Me

Clinical Experience

As an MD student at SJUCHAS, I've gained comprehensive clinical experience across multiple specialties including Internal Medicine, Surgery, Pediatrics, Obstetrics & Gynecology, and Community Medicine.

My approach combines evidence-based practice with patient-centered care, emphasizing teamwork and compassionate communication to deliver optimal healthcare outcomes.

Core Competencies:
