Getting There

By Air

The nearest airport is John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK), approximately 25 km from the venue.

  • Taxi: ~45 min, $60-80
  • AirTrain + Subway: ~1h 15min, $10.75

Public Transport

The venue is easily accessible via New York City's extensive public transportation network.

  • Subway: B, D, F, M, N, Q, R, W trains to 34th St-Herald Sq
  • Bus: M5, M7, M20, M34

By Car

Limited parking is available at the venue and nearby parking garages.

  • Venue parking: $40/day
  • Nearby garages: $30-50/day
