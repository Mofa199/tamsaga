About The Event

The 15th TAMSA General Assembly and 17th International Scientific Conference brings together medical students, professionals, and researchers from across Africa and beyond.

Join Medical Professionals

Networking opportunities with healthcare leaders

Why Attend TAMSA GA 2025?

This year's conference focuses on the most pressing medical challenges and innovations in Africa, including infectious diseases, non-communicable diseases, medical education, and public health strategies. With over 50 sessions across 3 days, you'll gain insights that will transform your medical practice and research.
